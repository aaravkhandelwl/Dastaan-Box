What is Dastaan Box

A toy that carries your grandmother's voice. Forever.

Dastaan Box is India's first offline AI folk puppet — a Kathputli-inspired companion that captures a grandparent's voice and turns it into a living, interactive storytelling experience for children.

A grandparent speaks their stories, nuskhas, and wisdom into it once. The toy carries that voice forever — answering children's questions, telling stories at bedtime, teaching in their mother tongue.

How It Works

Simple enough for a 5 year old.
Deep enough to last a lifetime.

1
Child builds the puppet

2

Mirror stickers, colourful fabric — makes the puppet truly their own creation.
3

Presses the red button, speaks a story or nuskha in any language. Saved to the AI chip instantly.

Child asks questions
"Nani, phir kya hua?" — the AI continues the story naturally in response.

The Innovation

Three breakthroughs no one else has combined.

🧠
Offline Voice Memory AI
Edge AI chip processes speech entirely on-device. Records, learns, and responds in the grandparent's personal voice — no cloud, no latency, no internet bill.
💬
Open Conversational Response
Unlike voice recorders that play back fixed audio, Dastaan Box responds to open-ended questions mid-story — continuing the narrative in the grandparent's actual recorded voice style.
🌐
Bhashini Multilingual Stack
Built on India's own open-source language AI — Bhashini. Supports 22+ Indian languages natively. No dependency on foreign APIs or English-first models.
🔁
Reusable AI Module
The voice chip is the only electronics component. It's removable and transferable — when the puppet body wears out, the chip and all recorded memories move to a new body. Nothing is ever lost.

Why this is patentable

The combination of offline multilingual voice personalisation + open-ended AI story continuation + Indian folk puppet form factor is a genuinely novel mechanism — no prior art exists in this exact configuration.

Three moats protect it: the patent on the voice-personalisation mechanism, the growing cultural knowledge database that takes years to replicate, and the emotional moat — once a family's grandmother's voice lives inside the toy, no competitor can give that back.
